In-Home Family Services Scenarios

Demonstrate your thinking through real-world situations

About These Scenarios

These scenarios present realistic situations you may encounter as a Family Life Specialist doing in-home family services. Read each scenario carefully and provide thoughtful responses.

There are no "right" answers - we want to understand how you think through complex situations involving trauma, addiction, crisis, and family dynamics.

Time: 45-60 minutes | Scenarios: 6
